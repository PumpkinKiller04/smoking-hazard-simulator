# smoking-hazard-simulator
A serious interactive educational tool that simulates health, social, and legal hazards of smoking. Can be used as an educational alternative punishment for smoking control enforcement.

A serious interactive educational tool that simulates the health, social, and legal hazards of smoking. Can be used as an educational alternative punishment for smoking control enforcement.

## ✨ Features

- **Three-dimensional Hazard System**: Health, Social, and Legal modules
- **Data-driven Scientific Model**: Based on WHO, CDC, IARC data
- **Serious Design Language**: No gamification, focused on educational impact
- **Educational Alternative Punishment Mode**: 5-minute learning as an alternative to fines
- **Multi-country Legal Simulation**: Laws from 10 countries including China, USA, UK, Singapore, etc.
